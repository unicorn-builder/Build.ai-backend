WHAT WAS IMPROVED
================================================================================

1. ENERGY BASELINE PRECISION
   ├─ Before: Hardcoded 120 kWh/m²/yr for all projects
   └─ After:  Country-specific baselines (95-130 kWh/m²/yr)
       • Senegal:        120 kWh/m²/yr (hot-humid)
       • Côte d'Ivoire:  110 kWh/m²/yr (hot-humid)
       • Morocco:        130 kWh/m²/yr (hot-arid)
       • Nigeria:         95 kWh/m²/yr (hot-humid)
       • Ghana:          100 kWh/m²/yr (hot-humid)

2. CLIMATE-AWARE CALCULATIONS
   ├─ Before: Same ventilation/insulation credits everywhere
   └─ After:  Climate-adapted energy contributions
       • Hot-humid zones: Better ventilation (4-9%), lower roof benefit
       • Hot-arid zones: Lower ventilation (2-7%), major roof benefit
       • Result: ±2-3% difference in final scores by climate

3. WATER FIXTURE-LEVEL ACCOUNTING
   ├─ Before: Lumped water savings in broad categories
   └─ After:  Fixture-specific consumption models
       • Dual-flush WC: 6L vs 9L standard → 13% savings
       • Low-flow faucets: 6L/min vs 12L/min → 8% savings
       • Rainwater harvesting: Up to 8% depending on rainfall

4. MATERIAL EMBODIED CARBON FACTORS
   ├─ Before: Generic "8% concrete savings" credit
   └─ After:  Material-specific factors
       • Concrete class influence (C20 -5% vs C30 baseline, C40 +5%)
       • Recycled steel (90% typical) = -75% embodied energy
       • GGBS concrete 30% = -25% cement carbon
       • Hollow blocks = -40% material volume vs solid

5. EDGE CERTIFICATION TIERS
   ├─ Before: Only "EDGE Basique" vs "Non certifiable"
   └─ After:  Three distinct levels
       • EDGE Certified:   All pillars ≥20%
       • EDGE Advanced:    All pillars ≥40%
       • EDGE Zero Carbon: Energy 100% + Water/Materials ≥40%
